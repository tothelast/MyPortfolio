More About Me


Childhood I was born when my parents had just moved from Yerevan, Armenia to Saint Petersburg, Russia. With my genetic makeup consisting of 75% Armenian and 25% Ukrainian, growing up in Russia was challenging for me as I stood out both physically and culturally. My body was slender and frail compared to others, and I often struggled to defend myself on the streets. It was because of this that my parents enrolled me in a judo club at the age of six, just as I entered middle school.

Judo I fell in love with judo from the very start, as it gave me the ability to protect myself and feel confident, earning respect from those around me. Judo became the top priority of my life until the age of 17, when I won a bronze medal at the Russian Championships and earned my black belt.

First step in career development From a young age, I always knew I was ambitious and determined. However, I realized that becoming a professional athlete, especially in judo, would not lead me to the ambitious future I craved. I decided to explore the world of business by pursuing a degree in Switzerland and the United Kingdom. I spent a total of two years studying in Europe and decided to return to Russia to begin my career rather than continue my studies. Looking back, I recognize that my decision was made out of immaturity, as I believed I could become a successful professional while simultaneously self-educating and forgoing a formal university education.

Current state of mind Although I found moderate success in my career, the experience revealed that I lacked many critical skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career. However, this time I knew precisely what I needed to study. Observing the vast technological tools and equipment in the company, I realized the significant impact of advanced knowledge on any industry's efficiency. This realization drove me to return to college and obtain a degree in computer science. My ultimate goal is to become a professional in the field of computer science, with a particular interest in robotics, artificial intelligence, and automation. I am inspired to remain active, always striving to enhance my quality of life in the future.
