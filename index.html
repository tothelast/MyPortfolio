Who I am?

I'm an ambitious individual with a professional background in judo and a passion for computer science. Currently pursuing a degree in this field, I'm focused on robotics, artificial intelligence, and automation. I believe these technologies can greatly enhance our quality of life. My portfolio showcases my projects and achievements, highlighting my dedication to pushing the boundaries of computer science. One of my favourite mottos says: “Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever.” ― Mahatma Gandhi. Let's shape a brighter tomorrow together!

Working Experience

From a young age, I always knew I was ambitious and determined. However, I realized that becoming a professional athlete, especially in judo, would not lead me to the ambitious future I craved. I decided to explore the world of business by pursuing a degree in Switzerland and the United Kingdom. I spent a total of two years studying in Europe and decided to return to Russia to begin my career rather than continue my studies. Looking back, I recognize that my decision was made out of immaturity, as I believed I could become a successful professional while simultaneously self-educating and forgoing a formal university education.

Change in Mindset

Although I found moderate success in my career, the experience revealed that I lacked many critical skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career. However, this time I knew precisely what I needed to study. Observing the vast technological tools and equipment in the company, I realized the significant impact of advanced knowledge on any industry's efficiency. This realization drove me to return to college and obtain a degree in computer science. My ultimate goal is to become a professional in the field of computer science, with a particular interest in robotics, artificial intelligence, and automation. I am inspired to remain active, always striving to enhance my quality of life in the future.
